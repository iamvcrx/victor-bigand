Hi ! I’m Victor Bigand. I’m a sound designer passionate about music and sciences.

Quick overview

I’m a sound designer composing electronic music under the name « vcrx » and an informatic engineer for creation (theater, dance and music shows). I recently released my first EP « Introspection » and worked for a theater show on real-time lights reacting to music : « L’Odysée du cerveau ».

Alongside my scientific studies in acoustics and signal processing, I have developed multiple artistic musical activities, from learning classical violin to composing electronic music.

The various projects I work on often involve learning new methods and tools, and are very interesting thanks to the sharing with others.
